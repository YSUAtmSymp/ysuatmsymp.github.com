Teaching strategy

The opportunity to engage higher education in teaching is one of the biggest rewards and greatest privileges at the same time that I see in an academic career.
I believe that adeptly trained problem solvers will be able to overcome challenging projects with less difficulty. So my teaching will encourage students to work on individual as well as group projects to foster their problem-solving skills based on the lectures.


Air-sea interaction (ATM2106)

This course introduces the basics of interaction between atmosphere and ocean, and explore its role in the earth climate.
It is offered in 2018 spring in the Department of Atmospheric Sciences at Yonsei University, Seoul, South Korea.


Ocean modeling (ATM9107)

This course is designed to provide students the background of ocean modeling and hands-on learning environment for their own research. It is offered in 2018 spring in the Department of Atmospheric Sciences at Yonsei University, Seoul, South Korea.
